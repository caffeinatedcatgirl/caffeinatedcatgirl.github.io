Who Am I?

I am a name and a title.
I am everything I share and all that I hide.
I am what I love and what I fear.
I am the sum of all my years.
I am a question asked by the universe itself.
I am star stuff and great... yet I am small.
I am a moment in time; here for you, but I’m going to get mine.
I am a thinker and a lover; not much of a fighter.
I am so much more than what you see; Absolem, you tell me!

Who Are You?

How am I to know?
I have written words of truth
as to my identity
both wise and uncouth.
But to shed light in your mind?
To explore parts of your soul
buried deep inside, left alone,
or ignored for all time.
To answer your inquiry
I have not the ability.
For what I see is nothing
more than a projection
crafted by your mind;
naked for all mankind.
I implore what's important
not who we are, but that we are.
For in this moment we share.
Stop these wanderings.
There is no answer; only despair.
Ignore the impossible and so we may stare...
Into one another's heart and soul;
past the abyss of time and the facade of mankind.
Lost now we are, but together as one may we discover
a new truth both wise and uncouth!

My Heart's Dark Friend.

There you are my old friend.
My eyes have seen many things, but with you, all fades to darkness.
I haven’t felt your cold embrace in quite some time.
How I’ve missed your breathless kiss.
You are my comfort and my solace.
You are my fear and my pain.
Be mine once again.
Drag me into this dark place.
Let me fight until I’m exhausted.
Struggle with me until I pant and can resist no longer.
Circle me!
Envelope me!
Enter me!
Break me!
Hold me.

Double Double Toil and Trouble.

A cascade of ginger beer.
A glug of spiced rum.
A stone of ice.
Twirl my witch's brew.
Watch it dance and mix in a glass,
before mixing in my veins.
Oh, sweet nectar
let me lose my faculties.
Free me from rational thought.
Transform my blood and numb my pain.
No solutions are offered, but an escape to be claimed!
